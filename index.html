My Favorite Places to Visit

  • Brea, CA
  • Okeene, OK
  • Virginia Beach, VA

Brea, CA

My best friend lives in Brea and so I try to visit as often as I can. The weather is always perfect, I love checking out all the various plants that are native to the area, and enjoy time with my friend.

Okeene, OK

My family is from the Oklahoma region. I love the wide open spaces, the serenity of the rural life, and the kindness of the people here.

Virginia Beach, VA

I have family who resides in Virginia Beach, which makes it a top destination. I love walking along the boardwalk by the beach, discovering amazing restaurants, and spending time with the people I love.
